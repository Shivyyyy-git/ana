About

A bit about me.

Higher education professional based in Rochester, NY, with 8+ years across international student services, DEI programming, and community engagement.

I currently coordinate support and engagement programs for international students at the University of Rochester, where I design cultural and educational events, mentor a team of student workers, and partner with offices across campus. Before that, I supported the inaugural Vice President for Community & Belonging at Nazareth University, where I helped launch the institution’s first Latinx Heritage Month and its first campus climate and DEI curriculum surveys.

The throughline of my work is helping students feel seen and prepared — whether that’s through orientation, restorative practice, advising, or programs that bring people across difference into conversation. I’m particularly interested in how institutions translate values like belonging and inclusion into the everyday experience of being a student.

Outside of work, I serve on several university committees and continue to chair International Education Week. I’m always open to conversations about international education, DEI program design, and student affairs leadership.
