About

Building inclusive programs in higher ed.

I’m a higher education professional with 8+ years of experience spanning international student services, DEI programming, and community engagement. I specialize in designing inclusive programs, supervising and developing student staff teams, and driving cross-campus collaboration that advances student belonging and institutional goals.

Currently, I coordinate support and engagement programs for international students at the University of Rochester. Before that, I supported the inaugural Vice President for Community & Belonging at Nazareth University, where I helped launch the institution’s first Latinx Heritage Month and its first campus climate and DEI curriculum surveys.

The throughline of my work is helping students feel seen and prepared — through orientation, restorative practice, advising, or programs that bring people across difference into conversation.
